Why OT Yourself CIC?

Because support should feel like a conversation, not a diagnosis. We bring Occupational Therapy out of the clinic and into homes, schools, and community spaces where people actually live their lives.

We focus on safety, self-regulation, daily routines, and connection — not “fixing” people. We meet you where you are.

Ready to talk?

Whether you’re a parent, a school, or someone looking for support — let’s start with a conversation. No pressure. No judgement.
